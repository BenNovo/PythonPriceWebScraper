This is a basic price scraping tool. Using Selenium, requests are sent to Target,
Best Buy, Amazon, and Walmart to find the price of the inputted product. Taking advantage of
each company's respective product search algorithm, this scraper selects the first non-advertisement
item that is most relevant to the user's search to enable more efficient comparison shopping. For example,
try searching "Apple Air Pods Pro" and "A2084"
